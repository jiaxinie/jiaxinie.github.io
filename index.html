I am currently a visiting assistant professor at Georgia Institute of Technology working with Xiaoyu He.

From 2022 fall to 2024 Spring, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the Shanghai Center for Mathematical Sciences supervised by Hehui Wu. In 2022 summer, I was a short-term postdoc at the Max Planck Institute of Mathematics in the Sciences hosted by Raffaella Mulas. I received my PhD from the University of California San Diego, where I was fortunate to be advised by Jacques Verstraëte. Here is my CV.

My research interests lie primarily in extremal combinatorics, but I keep my brain open for any interesting math problems.
