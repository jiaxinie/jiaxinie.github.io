I am currently a postdoc at the Shanghai Center for Mathematical Sciences under the supervision of Hehui Wu. I received my PhD from the University of California San Diego, where I am very fortunate to be advised by Jacques Verstraëte. Here is my CV.

My research interests lie primarily in extremal combinatorics, but I keep my brain open for any interesting math problems. My Erdős number is 3.
